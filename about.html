About Sam Rabon

I've spent more than 20 years exploring how the body moves, heals, and grows stronger.

I've spent more than 20 years exploring how the body moves, heals, and grows stronger. My path started as a professional dancer, living and working in Australia, Japan, Portugal, Italy and France, and evolved into a lifelong practice of understanding movement as medicine.

Today I work as a certified private coach and movement therapist, guiding people of all ages through recovery and transformation. My approach blends functional movement, strength training and mindfulness to help clients restore balance, rebuild mobility and move with confidence. Each program is tailored to the individual, whether they are recovering from surgery, rebuilding after pregnancy or seeking to enhance performance.

Over the years I've built, run and sold my own studios in Sydney and Hollywood, trained Lagree instructors, and collaborated with clinicians, coaches and clients who trust me to bridge the space between rehab and performance.

Movement is my language. It's how I help people reconnect with their bodies and find freedom, strength and resilience in the way they move. Whether we're working privately, in the studio or online, my goal is always the same: to help you move with awareness, balance and ease for life.
