About — Los Angeles

Thirty years of watching how bodies actually move.

Sam Rabon is a functional movement and prehab specialist. Her work focuses on intelligent, preventative movement that supports joint health, balance, and lifelong resilience.

For more than thirty years, Sam has worked in studios, on stages, and in the rooms where people come back from injury. That range is the point. The body does not move differently depending on whether it is performing, training, or recovering, and the principles that hold a dancer together are the same ones that bring a client back after surgery.

Her approach is preventative before it is corrective. She looks for the patterns underneath the pain, the small things the body has stopped doing well, and rebuilds them in an order that lasts. The result is not a harder workout. It is a body that works in the world, in the ways life asks of it.

Sam trains a small number of private clients each day in Los Angeles, alongside group sessions, live online coaching, and an on-demand library that brings the same precision to people anywhere.
