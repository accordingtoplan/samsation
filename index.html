SamSation

A Practice in Real Life Movement

A method for bodies that still need to work in the world. Built over thirty years in studios, on stages, and in the rooms where people come back from injury.

SamSation is not a fitness programme. It is a practice. The aim is not a harder workout; it is a body that moves with more intelligence, for longer, with less need for maintenance later.

The method is organised around twenty-one things the body does in life — five foundations, five directions, four patterns, five forces, two organisers. We come back to each of them, on purpose, on a rhythm.

The operating principle is simple: undo before you add. Strength that is built on compensation is strength with a tax. We take the tax off first.
