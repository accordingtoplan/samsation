SamSation is not a fitness programme. It is a practice. The aim is not a harder workout — it is a body that moves with more intelligence, for longer, with less need for maintenance later.

The method is organised around twenty-one things the body does in life. We come back to each of them, on purpose, on a rhythm. The operating principle is simple: undo before you add.
