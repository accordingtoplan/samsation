Library — The method

SamSation is not a fitness programme. It is a practice.

A practice built around how bodies actually move in life, not in a gym. The method has four parts. Everything in the library sits underneath one of them.

01

Foundations

Five things the body must be able to do before anything else. Everything is built on top of these, in order.

02

Directions

How the body organises movement across planes and vectors, so it stays capable in every direction life pulls it.

03

Patterns

The repeating shapes that show up in every sport and every life. Train the pattern and the activity takes care of itself.

04

Forces

Load, momentum, gravity. Learning to work with them rather than against them is what keeps the body durable.

Put it into practice

The sessions that bring the method to life are on-demand, ready whenever you are.
